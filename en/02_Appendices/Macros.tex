\startchapter[title=Index of commands]

The commands discussed in this introduction are listed in this index. Some
are simply mentioned, almost in passing, in which case the page that
appears in the index indicates where they are mentioned.  But other
commands are the subject of some more detailed explanation.  In this latter
case, only the place where the detailed explanation begins is listed in the
index, although the command may be cited in other places in the
introduction as well.

Not included in the index:

\startitemize

  \item The \tex{stopSomething} that closes a construction previously opened
    with \tex{startSomething}, unless the text says something special about
    the \tex{stop} command, or is treated at a different location than the
    one where the corresponding \tex{start} command is.

  \item Commands aimed at generating symbols, all found
    in \in{Appendix}[app:symbols].

  \item In the case of commands that generate a diacritic or letter, and
    that have an upper case and a lower case version, to generate the upper
    case or lower case letter respectively, only the lower case version is
    included.

  \stopitemize

  \blank\midaligned{***}\blank

  \placemacro

\stopcomponent
